01 Tool One

A bridge between consuming and acting

The problem nobody is solving: the gap between finishing a piece of content and knowing what to do with it.

Content to Action

Zapo

Product Thinker

The gap between consuming and acting

Personal ProblemContent GapNo-code Build
Why I built this

I save a lot of content. Reels videos articles breakdowns. I save them because they feel important. I never go back to them.

Not because the content was bad. But because there was no bridge between finishing something and knowing what to do with it.

That gap between consuming and acting. That is what Zapo is built to close.

What it does

You give it any piece of content. A reel a video a link anything.

It tells you what it actually is, what it is really about, and one thing you can do right now.

No saving no organizing no thinking. Just a clear next step immediately.

02 Tool Two

The question you have when you are actually traveling

Every railway app shows you the data. None of them answer the question you actually have when you are on the platform.

Information to Clarity

Railway Tool

Product Thinker

The question you have when you are actually traveling

Travel ProblemReal-time HelpChatbot Layer
Why I built this

Every railway app shows you timetables and train status. That is not the problem.

The problem is what happens when you are actually traveling. Especially if you are going somewhere new.

You have questions no app directly answers. You do not need more data. You need one clear answer right now.

What it does

A chatbot layer built into a travel companion.

You ask a real question in plain language. It gives you a direct answer not a list of links.

There is also an offline game built in for when there is no network and you are just waiting.
