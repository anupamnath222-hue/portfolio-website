About Me

I don't start with content. I start with what needs to change in the buyer's mind.

I've spent 5 years working inside real brands across EdTech, D2C, F&B, media, coaching, and early-stage startups. Different categories. Different formats. Different audiences. But the same problem every single time. Content was being created consistently but it wasn't structured to drive decisions. It was filling a calendar not changing a mind.

That gap is what I fix. I come in before the content starts. I find the real question the brand should be answering. I build the positioning, the structure, and the system. Then I execute it end to end. Most people either think but don't execute or execute without thinking. I do both.

VSL Creative Strategy

Conversion Architecture for a Trading Education Creator

Why I Built This

A trading education creator needed one video to convert cold Instagram traffic into course purchases. I built the complete creative strategy behind it — narrative structure, emotional arc, hook logic, proof sequence, and close. Every decision came from one question. What does the viewer need to feel at this exact moment to take the next step.

There is a difference between making content and making content that converts.

After delivering the work I watched it back as a strategist and saw clearly what the execution could not fix. Not the cuts. Not the subtitles. The structure. The thinking that should have happened before the camera turned on. I rebuilt the entire creative brief from scratch. This document is proof that I understand that difference.

What I Built

A full creative strategy and post-delivery audit covering audience insight, narrative architecture, conversion breakdown by timestamp, the three highest-impact fixes, a rebuilt creative brief, and a rebuilt script structure from pattern interrupt to spoken CTA.

Narrative Architecture — Every section of the video mapped to a specific conversion job not just a content beat Conversion Audit — Timestamp by timestamp breakdown of where the video lost the viewer and exactly why Rebuilt Brief — The complete creative strategy document that should have existed before production started
