About Me

I don't start with content. I start with what needs to change in the buyer's mind.

I've spent 5 years working inside real brands across EdTech, D2C, F&B, media, coaching, and early-stage startups. Different categories. Different formats. Different audiences. But the same problem every single time. Content was being created consistently but it wasn't structured to drive decisions. It was filling a calendar not changing a mind.

That gap is what I fix. I come in before the content starts. I find the real question the brand should be answering. I build the positioning, the structure, and the system. Then I execute it end to end. Most people either think but don't execute or execute without thinking. I do both.
